Dr. Evelyn Hart, a brilliant astrophysicist, has disappeared under mysterious circumstances. Your task is to sift through the evidence and uncover the truth.

Solution

The encoded coordinates on Dr. Hart's notebook lead to a hidden location. The hooded figure is revealed to be a jealous colleague who staged her disappearance.
